Veloura Cart Surfaces V1
========================

The customization is disabled by default.
When enabled, one selected color and radius are applied to:
- each complete cart-item card, including its options;
- free-shipping and gifting cards;
- the order summary card;
- standalone cart options.

Inputs, selectors, product images and the checkout button keep their own appearance.
Optional border and shadow controls are also disabled by default.
